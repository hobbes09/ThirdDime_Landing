It is the third painting - Third of May

Artists are historians and their art the journal. Undeniably, art is not only an expression of beauty and creativity; it is also a reflection of the times.Events such as war impact artists even deeper.
The first painting is by Picasso where he depicts a massacre during the Korean war.
The second painting is by Otto Dix which represents a ruthless class struggle taking place in Germany.
The third painting is by Goya where he presents a scene from the Peninsular war.
The fourth painting is by Manet depicting the execution of Maximillian by a firing squad.
Goya's painting is the original inspiration.
